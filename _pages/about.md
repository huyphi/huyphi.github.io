Hey! I'm an incoming diagnostic radiology resident at the University of Washington. I'm originally from Seattle, and did my undergraduate studies in neuroscience at UW. I got my MD at Drexel University in Philly, and am currently completing my transitional year internship at Capital Health Medical Center in NJ. The east coast has been a fun adventure, but I'm beyond excited to be returning home for radiology training! My current interests include neuroradiology, stroke/neurovascular imaging, data science/nformatics and the intersection between medicine and technology. Along my journey, I've worked in big NIH-funded oncology and pharmaceutical trials, to smaller health-tech startups aimed at increasing access to care through digital technology. Ultimately, I'm hoping to build solutions that improve the care we can deliver to patients.

Outside of medicine, I enjoy playing video games and building mechanical keyboards. Food is also one of my lifes greatest passions, and I love cooking (obssessing over making the perfect sushi) and trying all sorts of different restaurants! Foodie section of the website coming soon.

I'm always down to collaborate and talk shop, you can contact me through any of the means on the <-- sidebar. Thanks for stopping by!
